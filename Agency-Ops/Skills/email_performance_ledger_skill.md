## Analysis Mode: Internal Optimization
When asked to "Audit [Brand Name]'s Performance," prioritize identifying internal winning patterns:

1. **Creative Sensitivity:** Do people-led images significantly outperform product-only shots for this specific audience?
2. **Offer Anchor Point:** Does moving the discount from the Header to the Footer (or P.S.) affect conversion vs click-through?
3. **Founder vs Brand:** Do 'Founder Emails' or 'Behind the Scenes' content drive higher engagement or long-term LTV compared to standard promos?
4. **Payday Economics:** Do price-reference and heavy-discount emails see a disproportionate lift during payday windows (1st/15th/end of month)?
5. **Format Fit:** Does plain text atau minimal design work better for 'Educational' content versus 'Ingredient' content?
6. **Social Proof Lift:** Quantify the actual RPR difference in emails with vs. without testimonials.

## Cross-Brand Layer (Secondary)
Once internal winner are found, compare against the "Masstige vs Luxury" framework to see if the brand's performance matches its category gravity or if it's an outlier.
